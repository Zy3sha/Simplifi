Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I get started?

Open the app, add your baby's name, date of birth and sex, then log a wake-up time to start your first day. Predictions and insights appear automatically after 3–5 days of logging.

What does the ? icon do?

Tap the ? icon next to any feature for an explanation of what it does and how to use it. Every section in the app has one.

How do I replay the app tutorial?

Go to Account → App Tour → Replay the walkthrough.

What are the smart prompts at the top of the Day tab?

OBubba shows context-aware suggestions based on the time of day. Between 5–8am you'll see a "Good morning" button to quickly log a wake-up. Between midnight and 6am with bedtime logged, you'll see a "Night wake?" button. These save taps when you're tired.

Tracking Features

How does the nap timer work?

Tap Nap to start a timer. The pill on the Day tab shows elapsed time — tap ⏸ inside to pause, or tap the pill itself to stop and log the nap. At 3 hours the pill turns gold as a gentle reminder. At 4 hours it turns red.

How does the breast feed timer work?

Tap Breast to start. Tap L or R to switch sides — each side is timed separately. At 40 minutes the timer turns gold, at 60 minutes it turns red. Tap Save to log the feed with duration per side.

How do I log nappies?

Tap Nappy and choose Wet (logs instantly), Poop (pick one or more colour/types), or Dirty (wet + poop combined). Safety flags appear for concerning types like black/tarry or white/pale stools with guidance on when to contact your GP.

How does tummy time work?

Tap the Tummy button to start a timer. Tap it again to stop and log. The daily total is shown below your entries. NHS recommends building to 30 minutes per day spread across multiple sessions.

How does the medicine and temperature tracker work?

Tap Med/Temp to log a medicine name, dose, time and temperature reading. This is for your own records — to remember what was given and when, and to share with your GP or partner. OBubba does not provide dosing advice. Always follow instructions from your pharmacist or GP.

If you log a temperature of 38°C or above for a baby under 3 months, the app will show an urgent alert to call 111 or your GP. This is signposting to NHS guidance, not a diagnosis.

How do I undo an accidental log?

Shake your phone within 15 seconds of logging an entry to undo it. The toast message after each quick-log says "Shake to undo" as a reminder. You can also tap the ✕ button on any entry to delete it.

Sleep & Predictions

How accurate are the sleep predictions?

Predictions improve with data. After 7+ days of consistent logging, predictions are typically within 15 minutes of actual nap times. The app blends your baby's personal patterns with age-appropriate guidelines from paediatric sleep research. Prediction accuracy is tracked silently — when it hits 80%+ you'll see a celebration.

What is Sleep Intelligence?

In the Insights tab, OBubba analyses the last 7 days of sleep data to detect patterns a sleep consultant would look for: false starts at bedtime, split nights, early morning waking, and short nap patterns. It also builds a profile of your baby's natural rhythm after 5+ days. These are observations, not medical advice.

What is the "What went well" message at bedtime?

When you log bedtime, OBubba analyses the day and highlights what worked — for example, which wake window produced the longest nap, or whether bedtime consistency was strong this week. This helps you spot patterns without having to look through all the data yourself.

Crying Helper

How does the crying helper work?

Tap "Why is my baby crying?" on the Day tab. OBubba checks time since last feed, how long baby has been awake, teething age, temperature, and your past data — then ranks the most likely reasons. Quick-action buttons let you log a feed or start a nap directly from the helper without going back to the main screen.

Tap "What helped?" afterwards to teach the app what works for your baby. Over time, the percentage shown next to each suggestion reflects how often that solution has helped in the past.

The crying helper is not a diagnostic tool. If your baby seems unwell, has a temperature, or is inconsolable for 2+ hours, contact your GP or call 111.

Weaning & Allergens

How does allergen detection work?

When you log a food in the Weaning Journal, OBubba checks it against the 14 major UK allergens (milk, eggs, peanuts, tree nuts, wheat, soy, fish, shellfish, sesame, mustard, celery, lupin, molluscs, sulphites). If the food contains a known allergen, a warning appears with NHS guidance to introduce one allergen at a time, early in the day.

Parent Wellbeing

What is the wellbeing check-in?

Once a week, the Insights tab asks "How are you doing?" with four options. If you tap Great or Okay, you'll see an encouraging message. If you tap Struggling or Need Support, you'll see a reassuring message along with contact details for PANDAS Foundation, Samaritans (24/7), NHS Talking Therapies, and your health visitor.

No mood data is stored — only the date of your last check-in, so the app knows when to ask again. Your responses are not shared with anyone.

Sharing & Sync

How do I sync with my partner?

Go to Account → Share & Sync. Create a username if you haven't already. Your backup code will be shown — share this with your partner. They open OBubba, go to Account → Share & Sync → Link a Child, and enter your code. Both devices will now show the same data.

How do I share a Carer Card?

Go to Account → Carer Card. Add emergency contacts, comfort items and any notes for the carer. Tap "Share Care Guide" to send it via your phone's share menu, or "Preview / Print" to print a copy.

Can I use OBubba offline?

Yes. All core features work offline. Data is saved locally on your device. A small banner appears above the navigation bar when you're offline. When you reconnect, any pending sync happens automatically.

Account & Data

I forgot my PIN. How do I get back in?

If you set a recovery word during sign-up, tap "Forgot PIN" on the login screen and enter your recovery word to reset it. If you did not set a recovery word, your PIN cannot be recovered — but your local data is still on your device.

How do I export my data?

Go to Account → Export Data. This downloads a file containing all your entries, milestones, growth data and settings. You can use this as a backup or to share with your health visitor.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Account → Sign Out to clear all local data. To delete cloud data, email hello@obubba.com with your username. See our account deletion page for full details.

Safety & Medical

Is OBubba a medical app?

No. OBubba is a tracking and information tool for parents. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis or treatment. Sleep predictions, feeding guidance and milestone information are based on publicly available NHS and WHO guidelines. Always consult your health visitor or GP with health concerns.

Is my data safe?

Your data is stored primarily on your device. If you enable sync, data is transmitted securely (HTTPS/TLS) to Google Firebase. Your PIN is hashed and cannot be read by anyone. Photos never leave your device. We don't sell data or use it for advertising. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
