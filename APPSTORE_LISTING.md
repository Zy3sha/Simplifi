## Description (4000 chars max)

OBubba was built by a tired mum at 3am who got fed up juggling 5 different apps.

It's everything you need in one calm, simple place — feeds, naps, nappies, sleep predictions, and a gentle guide that learns your baby's unique rhythm. Like having a sleep consultant and a best friend in your pocket.

**SMART TRACKING — ONE TAP**
- Log feeds (bottle, breast, solids), naps, nappies, medicine, temperature
- Breast timer with L/R side tracking
- Nap timer with Live Activity on Lock Screen & Dynamic Island
- Voice logging via Siri — "Log a feed in OBubba"
- Home screen widget with quick-log buttons

**PREDICTIONS THAT LEARN YOUR BABY**
- Nap and bedtime predictions based on your baby's actual patterns
- Wake window tracking with "nap window open" alerts
- Personal Rhythm mode learns from 7+ days of data
- Today's Plan shows the whole day mapped out
- Bedtime suggestions with "start routine" reminders

**SLEEP INSIGHTS (Premium)**
- Sleep consultant-style weekly analysis
- Detects false starts, split nights, early waking, overtiredness
- Tonight's Guidance — personalised bedtime + what to expect
- Three-drive sleep model (awake pressure, sleep debt, circadian clock)
- "Why is my baby crying?" ranked by data

**WEANING & NUTRITION**
- 77 recipes across 4 stages (puree + BLW methods)
- Allergen tracker for all 14 UK allergens
- Food reaction logging
- Milk vs solids balance tracker
- NHS-aligned weaning guidance

**GROWTH & DEVELOPMENT**
- WHO growth charts with percentile tracking
- Developmental milestones checklist
- Age-appropriate play ideas
- Developmental phases (Wonder Weeks-style) timeline
- Teething tracker with visual tooth chart

**MEMORY BOOK**
- Digital scrapbook with polaroid-style photos
- Add captions, stickers, and notes
- Shareable album links for grandparents & family
- Beautiful journal-style page layout

**APPOINTMENTS & REMINDERS**
- Appointment booking with travel time calculation
- "Time to leave" notifications using your location
- Smart reminders (after nap, after feed, at bedtime)
- Nappy change reminders with smart reset

**NIGHT MODE**
- Calm blue interface after bedtime
- One-tap night wake logging (under 10 seconds)
- Night wake analysis and trends
- Morning wake to start fresh day

**SHARING & FAMILY**
- Partner sync — both parents on the same page
- Carer Portal — babysitters and nursery can log entries
- Care Guide sharing with QR code
- Health Visitor report export (always free)

**WELLBEING**
- Weekly parent wellbeing check-in
- Gentle nudges ("Have you spoken to another adult today?")
- Real support resources when you need them
- Sound machine (white noise, rain, heartbeat, shush)

Built with love by parents who've been there. No ads. No selling your data. Just a hug from one tired parent to another.

## Privacy Nutrition Labels
