Medical Disclaimer

This section is important. Please read it carefully.

  • OBubba does not provide medical advice, diagnosis or treatment.
  • Sleep predictions, wake window guidance, feeding targets and developmental milestones are based on general guidelines and your baby's logged patterns. They are not personalised medical recommendations.
  • Temperature and medicine logging is for your own record-keeping only. OBubba does not provide dosing advice. Always follow instructions from your pharmacist, GP or the medicine packaging.
  • Fever alerts (such as "38°C+ in a baby under 3 months — call 111") are signposting to NHS guidance, not a diagnosis.
  • The crying helper analyses logged data to suggest possible reasons your baby may be unsettled. It is not a diagnostic tool.
  • Growth chart percentiles are calculated using WHO Child Growth Standards data. They are indicative only. Your health visitor or GP should interpret growth patterns.
  • Safe sleep guidance references The Lullaby Trust (UK) and the American Academy of Pediatrics. Always follow your own healthcare provider's advice.
  • First aid links within the app direct you to NHS and St John Ambulance resources. In an emergency always call 999 (UK), 911 (US) or 000 (Australia).

Always consult your health visitor, GP, midwife or paediatrician with any concerns about your baby's health, feeding, sleep or development.
