## Guideline 2.1(b) — Location of In-App Purchases — HOW TO ACCESS

In-App Purchases can be accessed from multiple locations inside OBubba. The simplest path for review:

**Path 1 — Direct from Account tab (easiest):**
1. Launch the app and complete quick onboarding (enter baby's name + date of birth — any values work for testing)
2. Tap the **Account** tab (bottom-right, profile icon)
3. Tap **"Try free →"** or **"See plans"** on the "OBubba Free" plan card near the top
4. The paywall modal appears showing Monthly (£4.99), Annual (£44.99), and Lifetime (£79.99) plans
5. Terms of Use, Privacy Policy, and auto-renewal disclosure are visible at the bottom of the paywall

**Path 2 — Tap any locked feature:**
- Tap the **Insights** tab → tap any "PREMIUM" badge on the "Tip of the Day" card or "{baby}'s Rhythm" card → paywall opens
- Tap the **Insights** tab → tap **Sleep** → scroll down — premium insights are marked with a lock icon and tapping opens the paywall
- Tap **Today** tab → **Send to Family** or **Schedule Builder** cards — tapping when free opens the paywall

Note that In-App Purchases are restricted by the Apple sandbox environment — please ensure the test account has been granted sandbox access and the Paid Apps Agreement is active on the Account Holder's side.
