Delete Your Account

You can delete your OBubba account and all associated data at any time.

Step 1 — Delete Local Data

  1. Open OBubba
  2. Go to the Account tab
  3. Tap Sign Out
  4. Confirm when prompted

This removes all locally stored data from your device including baby profiles, tracking logs (feeds, sleep, nappies), photos, milestones, growth data, appointments, reminders, pinned notes, medicine logs, carer card information and all app preferences.

Step 2 — Request Cloud Data Deletion

If you enabled cloud sync by creating a username and would like your Firebase data permanently deleted, email:

hello@obubba.com

Include your username so we can locate your data.

Cloud deletion covers your username, backup code, and all synced baby data including profiles, tracking logs, milestones, teething records, weaning journal and growth measurements. Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.

Your request will be processed within 30 days.

What If I Only Want to Remove One Child?

You can remove an individual child's data without deleting your entire account. Go to Account → tap the child's name → Delete Child. This removes that child's data from your device and from cloud sync.

Questions

If you have any questions about data deletion, contact us at hello@obubba.com.
