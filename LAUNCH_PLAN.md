> "Every baby app turns your phone into a floodlight at 3am. Mine goes dark the moment you log bedtime. Because nobody wants to blind their baby at 3am. OBubba. Link in bio."

### Script 6 — Cost
> "Baby apps charge £15 a month. Mine's £5. Or £29 forever if you're one of the first 100. Because I remember being a tired mum on maternity pay. OBubba. Link in bio."

### Script 7 — Partner sync

### Script 8 — Carer mode

### Script 9 — The honest one
