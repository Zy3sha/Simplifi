Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 March 2026

OBubba ("we", "us", "our") is a baby tracking and parenting companion app built by parents, for parents. We take your privacy and your family's data seriously.

1. Data We Collect

Baby tracking data: Feeds, naps, nappies, weight, milestones, activities, and notes you log. This data is stored on your device and optionally synced to our cloud backup (Firebase/Firestore) if you create an account.

Account information: If you sign up, we store your display name, email (if provided), and a family backup code. We use Firebase Authentication.

Location data (optional): If you grant permission, we use your location solely to calculate travel time to appointments you've added (e.g. GP visits, health visitor appointments). We use Apple MapKit for route calculation. Your location is used in real-time only and is never stored, shared, or transmitted to any third party. You can deny location access and manually set travel times instead.

Photos (optional): Photos you add to the Memory Book are stored on your device. If you create a shareable album, photos are uploaded to Firebase for sharing via a private link.

Usage analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used (e.g. which features are popular). This data is anonymous and aggregated. No personal baby data is included in analytics.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide baby tracking, predictions, and insights
  • To sync data between your devices and with your partner
  • To calculate travel time to appointments (location, when permitted)
  • To send local notifications (nap reminders, bedtime alerts, appointment reminders)
  • To improve the app experience through anonymous usage analytics

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or your baby's data with any third parties. Period.

Your data may be shared only in these cases:

  • Partner sync: If you share a family code with your partner, they can access the same tracking data.
  • Shared albums: If you create a shareable Memory Book link, anyone with that link can view the photos and captions you included.
  • Cloud infrastructure: Data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, Storage) which is governed by Google's data processing terms.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using SQLite and localStorage. If you enable cloud backup, data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and stored in Firebase's secure infrastructure. We do not have access to your passwords.

5. Location Services

OBubba requests "When In Use" location permission only. We use your location exclusively to calculate driving time to appointments using Apple MapKit. Your coordinates are sent to Apple's routing API and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We never store, log, or transmit your location to our servers or any third party.

6. Children's Privacy

OBubba is designed for parents/carers to track their baby's care. We do not knowingly collect data from children. All data is entered by the parent/carer. Baby names, dates of birth, and tracking data are stored solely for the parent's use.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Export all your data from the Account tab
  • Delete your account and all cloud data at any time
  • Revoke location permissions in your device Settings
  • Disable cloud sync and use the app offline only

8. Cookies & Tracking

OBubba does not use cookies. We do not track you across other apps or websites.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you in the app if any material changes are made.

10. Contact Us

Questions about your privacy? Email us at hello@obubba.com
