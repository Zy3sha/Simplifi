Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 March 2026

1. Who We Are

OBubba ("we", "us", "our") is a baby tracking application operated by OBubba. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the app's features:

  • Account information: A username and PIN you choose. No email address, phone number, or real name is required.
  • Baby information: Your baby's name, date of birth, sex, and tracking data you enter (feeds, sleep, nappies, weight, height, milestones).
  • Recovery word: An optional secret word you set to reset your PIN.
  • Anonymous authentication: We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication which assigns a random identifier to your device. This is not linked to your identity.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide the baby tracking features you use
  • Sync your data between devices and with your partner
  • Back up your data to the cloud so it can be restored
  • Generate personalised insights based on your baby's patterns

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the app's core functionality.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored locally on your device and synced to Google Firebase Cloud Firestore.
  • Firebase data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • Your PIN is hashed before storage — we cannot see your PIN.
  • Access to your cloud data requires your username and PIN, or your recovery word.
  • We do not store or have access to any biometric data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your data may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • Partner sync: If you choose to share a child's data with another user via a sync code, that user can view and edit the shared child's tracking data.
  • Service providers: We use Google Firebase for authentication, data storage and hosting. Firebase processes data in accordance with Google's privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

6. Children's Privacy

OBubba is designed for parents and caregivers to track their baby's development. The app does not collect data directly from children. All data is entered by the parent or caregiver. We comply with applicable child data protection laws including the UK Children's Code (Age Appropriate Design Code).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: All your data is visible within the app at all times.
  • Delete your data: You can delete individual entries, entire days, or your complete account from within the app.
  • Export your data: You can copy daily summaries and reports from within the app.
  • Withdraw consent: You can stop using the app at any time. Deleting the app removes all local data.

8. Cookies & Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand general app usage patterns (e.g. which features are used most). This data is anonymised and aggregated. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking.

9. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account or all your data, it is removed from our servers. Anonymised analytics data may be retained indefinitely.

10. Medical Disclaimer

OBubba provides general information and insights based on NHS and WHO guidelines. This is not medical advice. Always consult your health visitor, GP, or paediatrician for medical concerns about your baby.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at: privacy@obubba.com
