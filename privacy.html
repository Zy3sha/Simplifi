How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to operate the app and provide features such as:

  • Sleep predictions and analysis
  • Feeding tracking and guidance
  • Growth chart percentiles
  • Milestone and development guidance
  • Parenting insights and pattern detection
  • Syncing data between devices or with a partner

We do not sell, rent or trade your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

Lawful Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR we process your personal data on the following bases:

  • Consent — you choose to enter data into the app and consent to its processing for the purposes described above. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your data.
  • Legitimate interest — to operate and improve the app for your benefit. We have assessed that this does not override your rights or expectations.

Cloud Storage

If you enable syncing by creating a username, your data is stored in Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore).

Firebase servers are located within European infrastructure (europe-west2 — London).

Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Your PIN is hashed using a one-way algorithm and cannot be read or reversed by anyone.

Data Sharing

We only share data with:

ServicePurpose
Google Firebase Optional cloud syncing between devices

Google processes this data as a data processor under their Data Processing Terms. Google does not use your Firebase data for advertising.

If you share your backup code with a partner, they will have full access to all synced data for that child. Only share your code with people you trust.

We do not share your data with any other third party.

Data Retention

Your data remains stored until you delete it.

  • Local data — remains on your device until you clear it via Account → Sign Out, or by clearing your browser data.
  • Cloud data — remains in Firebase until you request deletion by emailing us.

Your Rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to:

RightHow to exercise it
Access — see what data we hold Use Export Data in the Account tab, or email us
Correction — fix inaccurate data Edit any entry directly in the app
Deletion — erase your data Go to Account → Sign Out to clear local data. Email us to request cloud data deletion.
Portability — receive your data in a readable format Use Export Data in the Account tab (exports JSON/CSV)
Withdraw consent — stop processing Delete your data via the app or email us
Complain — raise a concern with a regulator Contact the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk or call 0303 123 1113

To exercise any of these rights email hello@obubba.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

OBubba is designed for use by parents and guardians aged 16 or over. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children. All data about babies is entered by their parent or guardian.

For users in the United States: we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Data about your child is provided by you, the parent, with your full knowledge and consent. We do not collect data from children directly, and we do not use children's data for advertising or profiling.

If you believe data has been entered about a child without parental consent, please contact us at hello@obubba.com and we will delete it promptly.

Medical Disclaimer

OBubba is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis or treatment.

Information in the app is based on publicly available guidance from organisations including the NHS, WHO, The Lullaby Trust and paediatric sleep research. OBubba is not affiliated with or endorsed by any healthcare provider.

Always consult your health visitor, GP or paediatrician with any concerns about your baby's health, feeding, sleep or development.

Changes to This Policy
