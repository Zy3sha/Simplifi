3. Where Your Data Is Stored

On your device (primary): All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This is the default — no data leaves your device unless you choose to enable sync.

Cloud sync (optional): If you enable Share & Sync, your data is transmitted to and stored on Google Firebase (Firestore) servers. Google acts as a data processor under their Data Processing Addendum. Firebase servers may be located in the United States and the European Economic Area. Data transfers to the US are covered by Google's Standard Contractual Clauses.

Photos: Photos remain on your device only. They are not uploaded to cloud servers.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing
  • We do not use your data to train AI models
  • We do not use cookies (localStorage is device-only, not transmitted to servers)

5. Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to collect anonymised, aggregated usage data such as which features are used most frequently. This data cannot identify you or your baby. You can disable analytics by disabling JavaScript or using a browser extension that blocks Firebase Analytics.

6. Data Retention

Local data is retained on your device until you delete it. Cloud-synced data is retained until you remove your backup code or request deletion. Analytics data is retained for 14 months in aggregate form, in line with Google's default retention period.

7. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct any inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (CSV export is available in the app)
  • Restriction — request we limit processing of your data
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact privacy@obubba.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Individual entries: Swipe or tap delete on any entry within the app
  • Cloud data: Remove your backup code in Account → Share & Sync
  • All local data: Clear website data for this site in your browser or device settings
  • Complete erasure: Email privacy@obubba.com with your backup code and we will delete all associated cloud data within 30 days

9. Children's Privacy

OBubba is designed for parents and carers to track their baby's information. The app is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. All baby data is entered by, and under the control of, the parent or carer.

10. Security

Data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Cloud data is protected by Firebase security rules that restrict access to authenticated users. We use anonymous authentication — no passwords are stored. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
